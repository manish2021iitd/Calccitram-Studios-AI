Process

From Idea to Export
in 4 Simple Steps

01 / INPUT
📝
Enter Your Prompt, Script or Audio
Type an idea, paste a full script, upload a blog post, podcast episode or PDF. Even a URL works. Any input format becomes your starting point in seconds.
02 / GENERATE
🤖
AI Builds Your Storyboard
Powered by Veo 3.1, Sora 2 & proprietary models, the AI instantly creates a complete storyboard — images, B-roll, narration, captions, music and transitions.
03 / REFINE
✨
Edit with Magic Commands
Type commands like "delete scene 3", "change narrator to female Indian accent", or "animate in cyberpunk style" and watch changes apply instantly. No timeline skills needed.
04 / EXPORT
🚀
Export & Publish Anywhere
Export HD 1080p or 4K. Choose 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok & Reels, 1:1 for Instagram. Translate into 160+ languages with one click. Watermark-free on paid plans.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long can my videos be? +
Free plan supports up to 5 minutes per video. Creator plan goes up to 15 minutes, and Pro Studio supports full 30-minute long-form videos — ideal for documentaries, courses and detailed explainers.
How does character consistency work? +
Upload a photo to lock in your character's face, outfit, and expression. Our proprietary AI engine maintains that exact identity across every scene — even in 30-minute videos — with no face drift or character shift.
What languages are supported? +
ChalachitramStudios AI supports 160+ languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, English, Japanese, Spanish, Arabic and more — with AI lip-sync aligned to the translated audio.
Can I use my own voice? +
Yes. Pro Studio plan includes AI voice cloning — record a sample of your voice and the platform creates a digital clone that narrates your videos in your exact tone, available in 160+ languages.
What aspect ratios are supported? +
All major ratios: 9:16 for TikTok, Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts; 16:9 for YouTube & LinkedIn; 1:1 for Instagram feed posts; and 4:5 for Facebook. Switch between them with one click.
Is the free plan actually free? +
Yes — no credit card required. The free plan gives you enough credits to create roughly 4 complete videos per month. Paid plans unlock longer videos, more styles, no watermark and higher export quality.
