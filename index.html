Process

From Idea to Export
in 4 Simple Steps

01 / INPUT
📝
Enter Your Prompt or Script
Type a simple idea, paste a full script, a blog post, or upload a podcast episode. Any form of input works as your starting point.
02 / GENERATE
🤖
AI Builds Your Storyboard
The AI instantly creates a complete storyboard with generated images, B-roll footage, narration, captions, and transitions matched to your content.
03 / REFINE
✨
Edit and Animate Scenes
Use Full Motion to animate static scenes with one click. Re-prompt specific visuals to swap clothing, scenery, or characters. Fine-tune timing and captions.
04 / EXPORT
🚀
Export in Any Format
Export in HD 1080p or 4K. Choose your aspect ratio — 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok and Reels, or 1:1 for Instagram. Watermark-free on paid plans.
